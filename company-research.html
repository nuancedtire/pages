Company research

Heidi: what to know

Heidi is positioning itself beyond an AI scribe: an AI Care Partner spanning documentation, evidence retrieval, communications and clinical-grade capture hardware.

73m+

patient consults supported in 18 months; over 2m consults weekly across 116 countries and 110 languages, per Heidi’s Series B announcement.

$65m

Series B led by Point72 Private Investments; total funding nearly $100m and valuation reported at $465m.

NHS

UK momentum includes Modality Partnership, Cambridge University Hospitals and pilots across NHS provider collaboratives.

Products and activities

Heidi Scribe

Ambient documentation: captures encounters and generates structured notes, referrals, care plans and other materials; clinicians review/edit/approve before record entry.

Heidi Evidence

Point-of-care medical research/evidence search; useful interview angle: ask how they evaluate source quality, recency and clinical safety boundaries.

Heidi Comms

Coordinates patient communications including calls, bookings, reminders and follow-ups; relevant to workflow automation beyond the note.

Heidi Remote

21g wearable microphone for consistent clinical audio capture, offline use and later sync; built to reduce reliance on phone/laptop capture in noisy wards, clinics and EDs.

UK governance angle

Heidi’s UK compliance page says it adheres to DCB0129, DTAC, DSPT, Cyber Essentials, the Data Protection Act and GDPR, and highlights UK data hosting. NHS practice pages commonly emphasise consent, clinician review and temporary/non-permanent audio handling.

Interview angle: “I’m interested in how Heidi treats safety, auditability, clinician-in-the-loop review and deployment governance as product requirements rather than afterthoughts.”
